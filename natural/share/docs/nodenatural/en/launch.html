Launching apps

In the left panel (generally, in blue) you can click the "Applications" icon, it will open a panel with two sections: in the top, you can start to typing the application name (inclusive without clicking the text field) and press "Enter" (or clicking "Search") and all applications containing these words will be shower below, then just click the applicationg that you want to open. After a search, you can start to typing again for search other applications, or press "Enter" (or clicking "Done") when the search field is empty for go back to the all-apps screen.

Instead, you can scroll on the all-apps screen and click the application that you want to open.
